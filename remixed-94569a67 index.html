How it works

Three steps to a perfect reply

01

Open any reply box

Visit Gmail, LinkedIn, or X. Click into any reply box and Reply Assistant appears automatically below it.

02

Pick your tone

Choose Professional, Friendly, Concise, Witty, or Empathetic. Claude crafts your reply in under 3 seconds.

03

Insert & send

Click "Insert into reply" and it drops straight into your compose box. Regenerate anytime for a different angle.

Installation

Add to Chrome in 60 seconds

01

Visit the Chrome Web Store

Click here to open Reply Assistant on the Chrome Web Store and click Add to Chrome.

02

Pin it & add your API key

Click 🧩 in Chrome's toolbar, pin Reply Assistant, then paste your key from console.anthropic.com.

03

Start replying smarter

Open Gmail, LinkedIn, or X. Click any reply box — Reply Assistant appears. Pick a tone, generate, insert. Done.
